Attendance Project

The attendance project was my personal project.
I was redesigning the attendence system based on the problems I noticed in the current one.
I learned many things from each one and those learnings helped me grow as a designer.
From the favicon project I learned how to make images and image prototypes better and grew by growing more confident in my abilities.
From the Data Story I learned all about how to use Google Sheets to make better data visualizations.
From my personal Attendence redesign, I learned the importance of making a POC (Proof of Concept) because I was working on it, then I hit a snag. This means that I can make it better before I get feedback and I can also convince people to get interviewed because I can show I can make the alternative attendence system.
